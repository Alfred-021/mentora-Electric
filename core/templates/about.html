About Mentora Electric

Your trusted partner in electronics solutions since 2025

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Mentora Electric has been at the forefront of providing comprehensive electronics solutions to individuals, businesses, and educational institutions. Our journey began with a simple mission: to make quality electronics components and services accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

We strive to empower innovation by providing high-quality electronic components, professional services, and expert guidance to electronics enthusiasts, students, and professionals alike.

Our Values

Quality

We source and provide only the highest quality components and materials for all our products and services.

Reliability

Our customers trust us to deliver consistent, reliable service and support for all their electronics needs.

Innovation

We stay at the cutting edge of electronics technology to bring you the latest components and solutions.

Community

We believe in building a strong electronics community through knowledge sharing and support.

Our Services

From PCB design and fabrication to laptop and mobile repair, we offer a comprehensive range of electronics services:

  • Professional PCB Design & Layout
  • High-Precision PCB Etching
  • Complete PCB Fabrication
  • Laptop & Mobile Device Repair
  • Component Sourcing & Supply
  • Electronics Consultation

Customer Support
